Frequently Asked Questions

Planology Frequently Asked Questions.

What is Planology?

Planology.com is the first all-direct interactive search engine for meeting and event planners and leisure travelers who are looking for the products and services you sell.

What is Planology Pages?

Planology Pages is our proprietary group sales accelerator that can instantly be connected to your existing website and Planology to help you generate more profitable direct group sales in less time and at less cost.

Which membership best aligns with my company's needs?

Planology targets both leisure travelers and professional meeting and event planners. Our Pro membership fits the needs of most businesses and lets them feature a combination of up to 20 products or services to both audiences. The Premium membership lets companies feature a combination of up to 30 products or services. Both memberships include the Direct Group Sales boost of Planology Pages. An Advertising membership provides you with space for two great product or company descriptions and the ability to have eye-catching affordable ads linked to your brand or products.

Do I pay commissions or other fees on sales?

No. All transactions take place directly between you and your customers.

How easy is it to show my products on Planology.com?

It takes about 5 minutes to showcase each of your products or services and feature a great image. It couldn’t be easier. Plus, we offer weekly classes on setup, tips, and sales.

What is Activitystore.com?

Activitystore.com is our purely leisure-focused search engine where anyone can instantly connect with you to buy your leisure products and services before arriving at your destination, as well as on-site. Every leisure product description, image, and ad placed on Planology.com is instantly featured on Activitystore.com at no additional charge. You don’t have to do anything.

How easy is it to update my information?

You can update your product information, and pricing, and refresh your ads, in minutes

Do I have to utilize Planology Pages to receive group sales RFPs?

No. Planology has a built-in Group Sales RFP/Order tool that planners can use to send you instant RFPs as they search Planology for their products and services.

Is there training for me and my staff?

Yes. Setup, support, and education sessions are offered weekly. It is incredibly easy.

Can I offer and feature Specials or Sales?

Yes. Our Hot Deals & Dates function allows you to feature as many “Specials” as you want to offer.

Money Back Guarantee?

Each account comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are unsatisfied with Planology in the first 30 days, we will close your account. You will not be charged. At the end of 30 days, you will be charged for whichever account you choose.
